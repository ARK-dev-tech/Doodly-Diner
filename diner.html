Drawn From Passion

The Doodly Diner was founded on the simple idea that food should be fun, fresh, and slightly imperfect—just like a good doodle. We believe in scratch-made recipes, locally sourced ingredients, and a dining experience that lets you relax and be creative.

Every plate is a canvas, and every meal is a small, edible work of art. Come see what we've sketched up today!
